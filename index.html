Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for the MA Psychology coaching?

Eligible candidates must hold a Bachelor's degree (B.A./B.Sc.) in Psychology or behavioral sciences from an accredited university. Final year students can also apply conditionally.

Are classes live or pre-recorded?

All core modules feature live classrooms with direct interactions. Recorded playbacks are uploaded to the Student Portal within 2 hours of session completion for revision.

Is study material included in the course fee?

Yes, comprehensive, well-structured, exam-focused digital study material and printed booklets are provided as part of the enrollment fees.

About Psychology Sphere

Psychology Sphere is India's leading competitive coaching portal and academic center dedicated to psychology entrance coaching. Founded with the mission to bridge the gap between academic theory and entrance exam patterns, we provide standard classroom curriculums led by highly qualified and experienced mentors.

We emphasize rigorous practice papers, conceptual clarity sessions, mock examinations, and comprehensive student support to help candidates crack prestigious national level entrance examinations such as UGC NET, MA Psychology entries, CUET PG, and TISSNET.
